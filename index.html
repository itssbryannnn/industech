College of Industrial Technology

BatStateU The NEU Lipa

The pioneer college established in BatStateU Lipa since 2000 that has produced more than 800 well-rounded globally competitive professionals who significantly contribute to the manpower resources in response to the rapid industrialization of the modern world.

Majors About

Program Offerings

Computer Technology

The program is a strong combination of theoretical and practical concepts in electrical and electronics technology, computer technology, mathematics, computer science, management and general education that leads to the Bachelor of Industrial Technology degree.

See Curriculum

Electrical Technology

The program will prepare graduates with the skills necessary to enter careers in the design, application, installation, manufacturing, operation and/or maintenance of electrical systems.

See Curriculum

Electronics Technology

The program provides a thorough understanding of digital electronics, circuit analysis, electronic devices, machine controls, programmable logic controllers and industrial electronics. This course also includes theoretical analysis, software simulation and hands-on applications.

See Curriculum

Instrumentation and Control Technology

The program prepared graduates with the technical and managerial skills necessary to enter careers in design, manufacturing, marketing, operations and maintenance in the field of measurement, control, robotics and automation technology.

See Curriculum
